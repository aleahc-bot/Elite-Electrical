Our Story

Built by people who show up. Here's how a three-person crew became one of Southwest Florida's trusted names in power and cool air.

Elite Electrical Contractors opened in September 2001 with a single crew of three and one promise: do the job right, and show up when you say you will.

That promise built a reputation. Word travelled, the crews grew, and today Elite is more than a hundred people serving residential, commercial and industrial customers across the region.

Along the way, air conditioning and refrigeration joined the electrical side — so one company could keep a Florida property both powered and comfortable.
